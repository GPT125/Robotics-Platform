## ⚠️ Before you publish — fill these in

Each document uses the placeholders below. Search-and-replace them across all three files:

| Placeholder | What to put |
| --- | --- |
| `[COMPANY_LEGAL_NAME]` | The legal entity that operates the app (e.g., your name, LLC, or company). |
| `[CONTACT_EMAIL]` | A monitored support/privacy email (e.g., `privacy@yourdomain.com`). |
| `[GOVERNING_LAW]` | The state/country whose law governs (e.g., "the State of Arizona, USA"). |

## ⚠️ Not legal advice

These are honest, good-faith templates written to match what the app actually does. They are **not legal advice**. Because the app is used by students (including minors) and submitted to the Apple App Store, have a qualified attorney review them before launch, and confirm your obligations under **COPPA**, **GDPR/UK-GDPR**, **CCPA/CPRA**, and Apple's App Store Review Guidelines (esp. §1.3 Kids, §5.1 Privacy).
