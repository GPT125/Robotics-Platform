## 2. Scope and acceptance

This policy explains what we collect, why, and what your choices are when you use the MatchMind app and any related services (together, the "Service"). By using the Service you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

## 3. Information we collect

### 3.1 Information you provide
- **Account information:** your name, email address, and profile photo when you sign in with Google, Apple, or email/password. If you sign in with Apple, you may use "Hide My Email," and we will only receive the relay address Apple provides.
- **Profile choices:** display name, avatar (a preset icon or an image you upload), role (student, coach, or parent), preferred language, and appearance settings (such as accent color).
- **Team selections:** the VEX team number(s) you choose to follow (e.g., your own team; coaches and parents may follow multiple teams). Team numbers are public competition identifiers, but your *association* with a team is information you give us.
- **Content you create:** scouting notes (including ratings, tags, text, and attached photos/videos), to-do lists and tasks, favorites, teammate names/emails you enter for invitations, and — if/when messaging features are enabled — messages and attachments you send.
- **AI conversations:** the text you type or dictate to the AI assistant, and any images, video, or video frames you upload for analysis.
- **Voice input:** if you use voice input, audio is converted to text (by your device/browser's speech service) and the resulting transcript is processed like typed text. We do not keep raw audio recordings.

### 3.2 Information collected automatically
- **App settings and local data:** preferences and content may be stored on your device (local storage) so the app works offline and remembers you.
- **Basic technical data:** device type, app version, language, time zone, crash and error information, and approximate region inferred from your network — used to run, debug, and improve the Service.
- **Notification tokens:** if you enable push notifications, we store the device token needed to deliver them.

We do **not** collect precise GPS location. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs and we do not show targeted ads.

### 3.3 Information from other sources
- **RobotEvents (public competition data):** teams, events, match schedules and scores, rankings, skills results, and awards. This data is public competition information, not data about you personally, but it may include your team's number and performance.
- **Sign-in providers:** Google and Apple provide your name, email, and (for Google) profile photo when you authorize sign-in.

## 4. How we use information

We use the information above to:
- Create and operate your account and persist your data across devices;
- Show your selected team(s)' events, matches, scores, rankings, and awards;
- Power features you invoke: scout notes, to-do lists, favorites, alliance suggestions, match analysis, and the AI assistant;
- Send notifications you've enabled (e.g., match results for your team) and emails you request (e.g., teammate invitations);
- Apply your language and appearance preferences;
- Maintain safety (e.g., content filtering in any chat features), prevent abuse, and enforce our Terms;
- Debug, secure, and improve the Service;
- Comply with legal obligations.

We do **not** sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own advertising.

## 5. AI features — important disclosures

- When you use the AI assistant (including image/video analysis and alliance recommendations), your prompts, relevant app context (such as your team number, upcoming matches, rankings, and scout notes you've saved), and any media you attach are sent to **third-party AI providers** (which may include Groq, OpenAI, OpenRouter, DeepSeek, or similar providers we configure) to generate a response. These providers process the data under their own terms; we configure them for service provision, not for advertising.
- To ground answers, the Service may also retrieve **public web content** (for example, VEX Forum threads and official manual pages) and show you the sources.
- **AI output can be wrong.** Predictions are statistical estimates, not facts. Rules answers are not official rulings — only the official game manual, the Q&A system, and event referees are authoritative. Do not submit confidential or sensitive personal information to the AI.
- If a prediction or analysis turns out wrong, we may store that outcome (match ID, predicted vs. actual result) to improve future estimates. This is competition data, not personal data.

## 6. Children's privacy (COPPA)

The Service is intended for users **13 and older**. Robotics teams include younger students; if you are under 13, you may only use MatchMind with verifiable consent from a parent/guardian or through a school/organization authorized to consent on a parent's behalf, where permitted by law.

- We collect only the information reasonably needed to operate the features described above.
- Parents/guardians may review, request deletion of, or refuse further collection of their child's personal information by contacting **[CONTACT_EMAIL]**. We will verify your identity as the parent/guardian and respond as required by law.
- Any chat features are designed with minors in mind (e.g., limited identity display such as first name and team number, profanity filtering, reporting/blocking, and no public open-ended messaging). We do not permit users to make their personal contact information public through the Service.

If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without required consent, we will delete it promptly.

## 7. How information is shared

- **Within your team (your choice):** content you explicitly share with teammates (e.g., a shared to-do list or shared scout note) is visible to them, attributed to your display name and avatar.
- **Service providers (processors):** hosting and database (Firebase/Google Cloud), authentication (Firebase Auth — Google, Apple, email/password), email delivery (e.g., Resend or SendGrid) for invitations/notifications you trigger, push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging / Apple Push Notification service), and the AI providers described in Section 5. They may process data only to provide services to us.
- **Public competition data:** anything shown from RobotEvents is already public.
- **Legal and safety:** we may disclose information if required by law, or as reasonably necessary to protect users (especially minors), investigate abuse, or enforce our Terms.
- **Business transfer:** if MatchMind is acquired or reorganized, data may transfer to the successor under this policy.

## 8. Storage, retention, and deletion

- Data is stored on your device and, when you sign in, in our cloud database (Firebase/Google Cloud, which may store data in the United States or other countries).
- We keep your data while your account is active. You may delete individual content (notes, tasks, chats, favorites) in-app at any time.
- **Account deletion:** you can delete your account in Settings or by emailing **[CONTACT_EMAIL]**. We will delete or de-identify your personal data within 30 days, except minimal records we must keep for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes. Cached copies may persist briefly in backups before being purged.
- Guest mode stores data only on your device; deleting the app removes it.

## 9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards (encryption in transit, access controls, scoped database security rules, server-side storage of API secrets). No system is 100% secure; please use a strong password and keep your sign-in credentials private.

Other choices:
